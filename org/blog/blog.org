* How Knowing Physics Helps Learning Engineering :noexport:
- You'll come into engineering with the experience and grit that'll help you work with:
  - Taylor expansions
  - Differential calculus (gradients, div, curl)
  - Using differential element to derive equations
    - Dynamics
    - Stress-strain
    - Fluid dynamics
  - ODEs
  - Complex numbers and Euler's formula
- You'll learn the importance of formalisms
  - Proper units and dimensional analysis
  - Putting vector signs everywhere
  - Comprehensive diagrams
